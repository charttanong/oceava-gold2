Our Bluefin Tuna are caught in the pristine waters of the Mediterranean. We strictly adhere to sustainable fishing quotas to ensure the longevity of the marine ecosystem.

Cold Chain & Quality Control

Immediately upon landing, the fish enters a strict cold chain environment. Each tuna undergoes rigorous histamine testing and core temperature checks to guarantee Sashimi-grade safety standards.

Advanced Processing

Loins are processed with precision and treated with Tasteless Smoke (CO) technology to lock in the natural ruby-red color, followed by super-freezing at -60°C to capture peak freshness.

Precision Customization

Before delivery, frozen loins are expertly cut using band saws into your desired specifications: Steaks, or Cubes, ready for immediate culinary use.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ varies by product type. For fresh tuna loins, the MOQ is typically 50kg. For processed frozen goods, it's 100kg. Please contact sales for specific details.

Do you provide delivery nationwide?

Yes, we cover all major provinces in Thailand using temperature-controlled logistics partners to ensure freshness upon arrival.

How can I become a supplier partner?

We welcome suppliers who meet our strict sustainability and quality standards (MSC, HACCP). Please fill out the contact form with the subject "Other" to initiate a discussion.
